The Food

Flying ninja sushi and bar, is a japanese restaurant located at near Fishermans Wharf at the North Beach of San Francisco. All the guests like the menu that offers great Japanese cuisine at this restaurant. Enjoy nicely cooked miso soup, chirashi don and yakitori chicken that are proposed at Flying Ninja. It's time to try tasty baked alaska. It's easy to find this place due to the convenient location. You will be offered delicious wine, beer or lemon sake. Clients visit this spot to taste good tea. Many visitors find the staff knowledgeable. If you want to experience fine service, you should go to this restaurant. Tasty dishes at attractive prices are provided here. It's a good place with the comfortable ambiance. Google gives it 4.1 so you can choose Flying Ninja to spend a good time here.

The Review

We were absolutely blessed to happen upon this gem. We were greeted with a friendly smile and sat at the sushi bar. The fish was displayed beautifully and the sushi chef immediately made our selection - crisp white coat and brilliantly sharp knife.

We had the lunch chirachi selection - the fish selected was divine and a really lovely sampling. Miso soup some of the best I've ever had ( I worked in Japan for 2 years)....... The chef was delightful - and then proceeded to make us a dish of Crispy Salmon Skin with cucumber and chili that was his treat for us. This restaurant is truely an unbelievable place.
